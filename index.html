Max Salary Calculator

This calculator is designed for SURS-eligible employees who are at or approaching the maximum of their salary range. Use it to calculate your "overload" stipend based on designated or projected Cost of Living Adjustments ("COLAs") and view the resulting annual salaries that will be reported to SURS as your "period earnings". The calculator assumes that ranges will remain fixed for up to 5 years before the next adjustment.

Note: All calculations are performed locally in your browser. Your salary data is never transmitted or stored. Your browser's auto-complete feature may remember values you enter.
